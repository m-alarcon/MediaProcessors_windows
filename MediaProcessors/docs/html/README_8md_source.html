5 Dependencies:
6 =============
7 
8 MediaProcessors depends on the following third party libraries (concrete
9 version tested and license is specified in parenthesis):
10 
11 > cjson (1.5.8; MIT)<br>
12 > lame (3.99.5; LGPL)<br>
13 > LHE which is a FFmpeg adaptation (3.3; LGPL and GPL)<br>
14 > live555 (version dated July 18, 2017; LGPL)<br>
15 > mongoose (6.8; GPL)<br>
16 > nasm (2.14rc0; 2-clause BSD)<br>
17 > pcre (8.40 11; BSD)<br>
18 > sdl (2.0; zlib license)<br>
19 > unittest-cpp (2.0.0, MIT)<br>
20 > uriparser (0.8.2.; The 3-Clause BSD)<br>
21 > valgrind (3.13.0, GPL)<br>
22 > x264 (July, 2017 , GPL)<br>
23 > yasm (1.3.0, BSD)<br>

29 BUILD & INSTALLATION:
30 =====================
31 
32 OS required: Linux.<br>
33 Successfully tested on Ubuntu 16.04 LTS.
34 
35 MediaProcessors provides four libraries:
36 
37 1.- utils: Common tools used by the rest of libraries.<br>
38 Dependencies:
39 > uriparser
40 
41 2.- procs: Offers a generic interface for all multimedia processors. Multimedia
42 codecs and multiplexers are implemented as a particularization of the generic
43 processor interface (and in consequence, all codecs and muxers offer the same
44 generic interface/API).<br>
45 Dependencies:
46 > utils<br>
47 > uriparser<br>
48 > cjson<br>
49 > valgrind, unittest-cpp for unit-testing
50 
51 3.- codecs: Wrappers/implementations of several audio and video codecs.<br>
52 Dependencies:
53 > utils<br>
54 > procs<br>
55 > uriparser<br>
56 > cjson<br>
57 > lame<br>
58 > x264<br>
59 > FFmpeg libraries and its dependencies (nasm, yasm)<br>
60 > mongoose, valgrind, unittest-cpp for unit-testing
61 
62 4.- muxers: Wrappers and implementations of several multimedia
63 multiplexing/demultiplexing formats.<br>
64 Dependencies:
65 > utils<br>
66 > procs<br>
67 > uriparser<br>
68 > cjson<br>
69 > Live555 lbraries and its dependencies<br>
70 > mongoose, valgrind, unittest-cpp for unit-testing
71 
72 Each library can be configured and compiled as usual, providing a prefix if
73 desired. That is:
74 > $ ./configue<br>
75 > $ make clean; make all
